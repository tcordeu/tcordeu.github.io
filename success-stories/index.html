    How I helped Shipnow

    Shipnow is an Argentinean startup and, since its inception in 2014, it has simplified the shipment process of more than 300 clients. Shipnow takes the responsibility away from sellers of having to handle complex shipments across the whole country for each of their products. Through a simple yet fully featured web application and seamless integrations all major e-commerce platforms, clients can take their minds off shipment intricacies and focus on what they do best: selling....
